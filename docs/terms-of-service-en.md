## 4. The transparency model

### 4.1 Core principle

Zemichat's transparency model means that the Team Owner has full insight into Texters' communication. This includes:

- All messages that Texters send and receive.
- Deleted messages (marked as deleted but not removed).
- Message edit history.
- Call logs.

### 4.2 Super's privacy

Chats between Supers (adults) where no Texter participates are private and cannot be seen by the Team Owner. This separation is a fundamental part of the service's design.

### 4.3 Consent

By using Zemichat as a Texter, or by creating a Texter profile, you confirm that you understand and accept the transparency model.
